Spiral Holders

After I purchased my 3D printer I was itching for something to create. Inspired by the bland desk at my job void of my personal effects, I decided to design a pencil holder. Spiraling forms have a certain appeal to it, so by experimenting with modeling and the limitations of FDM printers I created these pencil holders.
