About Me

Mechanical Engineer / Industrial Designer

My name is Taajwar D'Van Howard, but you can just call me D'Van. I graduated from the University of Kentucky in 2014 with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. I then decided one wasn't enough, so to pursue a passion in design I now attend the University of Cincinnati currently pursuing a degree in Industrial Design (2020). I'm often curious how things work and am frequently annoyed by poor design. I want to make things that positively impact the world and this is my journey.
